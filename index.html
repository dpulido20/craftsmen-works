Selected work

Portfolio.

Custom Midcentury Entry Table

A refined mid-century inspired entry table designed to bring warmth and elegance into narrow spaces. Crafted with solid pine legs, a rich walnut top, and matching walnut drawers, the piece balances natural contrast with clean architectural lines.

Swivel Live Edge Oak Chair

A sculptural swivel chair crafted from a reclaimed live-edge oak log once destined to be discarded. Mounted on a smooth 360-degree swivel base, the chair blends rustic materiality with modern functionality.

Beach Inspiration

A lightweight pine chest and shelving combo inspired by relaxed coastal living and outdoor beachside spaces. Designed for easy transport and everyday functionality, the piece combines open shelving with practical storage.
